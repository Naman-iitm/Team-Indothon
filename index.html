⚡ What is IndoThon?

IndoThon isn’t just another hackathon — it’s a national battleground for innovation where hackers, dreamers, and builders unite to solve real-world problems using tech-driven creativity.

Open to all Indian college students, teams of 2–4 members collaborate, ideate, and prototype disruptive solutions. Whether you're a coder, designer, or visionary — IndoThon welcomes you!

Prepare for 48 hours of pure adrenaline, mentorship, networking, learning, and problem-solving brilliance.

💠 IndoThon Team Journey

🧠 Idea Origin

May 2025 – The concept of IndoThon was born with a vision to create an all-India beginner-friendly hackathon and coding community.

👥 Core Team Assembled

June 2025 – 11 enthusiastic students from different parts of India joined together, forming the founding team of IndoThon.

🌐 Website Launched

July 2025 – The official IndoThon website was launched, showcasing our mission, team members, and upcoming activities.

📣 Outreach & Awareness

July 2025 – Outreach efforts began through LinkedIn and other platforms to build collaborations, gain visibility, and attract early supporters.

🚀 Preparing for First Hackathon

August- September 2025 – The team is currently learning and preparing to participate in their first hackathon event.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is IndoThon?

➕

IndoThon is a student-led hackathon initiative that brings together beginners and passionate tech learners from across India to collaborate, build, and grow together.

Is this an official event?

➕

No, IndoThon is an independent student community initiative and not affiliated with any institution or sponsor. It is purely run by students, for students.

Who can join IndoThon?

➕

Anyone with a passion to learn and build — from any college, background, or experience level — is welcome to join us in our events and projects.

Do I need prior experience?

➕

Not at all! IndoThon is beginner-friendly. We provide learning resources, mentorship, and team activities to help you grow from scratch.

Are there any fees?

➕

No. IndoThon is a free and open community initiative. All our internal events and projects are free for participants.

What kind of projects does IndoThon team build?

➕

We work on real-world, beginner-to-advanced level projects in areas like web dev, AI/ML, sustainability, mental health, and more. Our goal is to learn by building together.

How can I join the IndoThon team?

➕

Currently, our core team is full. But we open collaboration forms for event volunteers, designers, developers, and community leads from time to time.

Is IndoThon open for collaborations?

➕

At the moment, we are focused on building our community and projects internally. We may consider collaborations in the future based on mutual learning goals.
