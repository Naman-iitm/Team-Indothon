⚡ What is IndoThon?

IndoThon isn’t just another hackathon — it’s a national battleground for innovation where hackers, dreamers, and builders unite to solve real-world problems using tech-driven creativity.

Open to all Indian college students, teams of 2–4 members collaborate, ideate, and prototype disruptive solutions. Whether you're a coder, designer, or visionary — IndoThon welcomes you!

Prepare for 48 hours of pure adrenaline, mentorship, networking, learning, and problem-solving brilliance.

FAQs

Who can participate?

All college students across India can register in teams of 2–4 members.

Is there any registration fee?

No, registration is completely free for all participants.

What is IndoThon?

IndoThon is India’s student-driven hackathon community, built to empower innovation through real-world problem solving, tech, and teamwork.

Who are the core team members behind IndoThon?

The founding team includes: Aryan (Founder & Head), Naman (Co-founder), Janhavi (Social Media Coordinator), Rahul (Web Admin), Shivam, Aakash, Gaurav, Ritika, Yukti and Moumita (Community Manager).

What kind of projects are expected?

We encourage tech-driven solutions across themes like AI/ML, sustainability, education, mental health, fintech, drone tech, and more.

Will mentors be available?

Yes! You’ll have access to experienced mentors from IITs, NITs, and industry during the hackathon to guide you through ideation to execution.

Are there any prizes?

Yes, winners will receive exciting cash prizes, internship opportunities, and goodies sponsored by top institutions and companies.

How can I stay updated?

Follow our official social media handles and join our Discord server (link on homepage) for all announcements, updates, and support.

Can first-year students participate?

Absolutely! Whether you're a fresher or a senior, everyone is welcome to showcase their ideas and learn through IndoThon.

Can I participate solo?

No. IndoThon encourages collaboration. Minimum 2 and maximum 4 members per team are allowed.

Will there be offline rounds?

The current edition is fully online. However, future editions may include hybrid/physical venues at partnering IITs/NITs.

Where is IndoThon based?

IndoThon is a pan-India initiative, originally founded by students from IIT Madras and passionate collaborators from across India.
